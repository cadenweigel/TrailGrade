How to Create Your Trail

Click on the map to add points to your trail. For best results, place points very close together to ensure a smooth path.

You can drag points to adjust your route. Click "Undo" to remove the last placed point.

Click "Edit Mode" to enable or disable moving points. When ON, you can drag points to fine-tune your trail.

When your trail is done, enter a trail name and click "Export GeoJSON" to save your trail.

Note: Your trail will be uploaded for review. If approved, it will be added to the TrailGrade database!

You can also upload an existing .geojson file below! Make sure that the file name is the name of the trail.
